Ways on how to prevent fire from happening in your house
Possible causes of house fire How to prevent it
Always double check the electricity plugs if you are going outdoors or before sleeping because an explosion can possibly happen with electricity overload.
Keep torches, lighters, matches, out of the reach of children due to the fact that they can play with it and can possibly cause a fire.
When you set up fires, for example, starting the cooker, it creates a fire. Distancing flammables can reduce the risk of spreading the fire. Examples of flammables are: paper, clothing, any type of wood
